Spaces

 
In Korean, an artist is 작가 (作家), meaning one who builds a house.




 
Clinic  |  Bibliotheca  |  Kitchen  |  Waterspout  |  Garden
 
 
My work always begins from an image that leads into a space. Where one space ends, another has already opened, like a threshold. One space becomes part of another; one becomes a passage into it; one becomes a way back; and another becomes the process of becoming the one.
I keep walking through these spaces and recording them, as so many do.
